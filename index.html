Brief Description

Reel and Riddles is a cozy fishing game where players catch fish by solving puzzles. Each fish will have its own rules variation, and players need to guess its movement sequence in limited attempts. Each attempt will provide hints on whether each guess move is in the sequence and is in the correct spot.

Features:

  • Engaging puzzle machanics
  • Over 15 unique fish species and rule variations
  • Gears that give players advantages in solving the puzzles
  • Maps set in various seasons and terrains
  • Cozy environment and relaxing music
